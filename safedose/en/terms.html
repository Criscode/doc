1. About SafeDose

SafeDose is a health-tracking mobile application that helps parents and guardians monitor a child's temperature, record administered medications and dosages, and store basic child profile information including name, weight, and year of birth. SafeDose is intended for informational and personal record-keeping purposes only.

⚠️ Medical Disclaimer SAFEDOSE DOES NOT PROVIDE MEDICAL ADVICE. The information or outputs generated by the App are not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek guidance from your physician or qualified health provider with any questions regarding your child's health. Never disregard professional medical advice because of something recorded in this App.

1.1 Emergency Situations

In the event of a medical emergency, call your local emergency services (e.g., 112 in the EU, 911 in the US) immediately. Do not rely on SafeDose in emergency situations.
